ABOUT ETHIK

It is no coincidence that Ethik is born in a land where non-violence is considered the greatest virtue. We are activists for a leather-free world and we will never buy the argument that animal skin must be used to amplify a human's fashion quotient or adorn their homes and cars.

Ethik is a Leather-free Fashion Label for men based in India's Silicon Valley, Bengaluru. Launched in 2013, Ethik has been the brand of choice for those who believe in making sustainable, conscious, cruelty-free stylish choices. Our shoes, belts, wallets & accessories are of superior quality, ethically crafted & built to last.
